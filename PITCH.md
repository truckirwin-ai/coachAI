## Executive Summary

**The Problem**

Professional development spend tops $350B annually. Most of it produces nothing measurable. Generic courses, passive video libraries, and one-size-fits-all LMS platforms don't change behavior — they just check a compliance box.

**The Product**

Coach AI is an AI-powered coaching platform built for working professionals. Each coach is a domain specialist — leadership, communication, strategy, execution — with a distinct voice, style, and curriculum. Users work through structured 4-week skill cycles combining:

- **TED-talk style video lessons** delivered by AI avatars (HeyGen LiveAvatar)
- **Podcast-style audio coaching** via ElevenLabs voice synthesis
- **AI evaluation** with rubric-scored feedback on applied practice
- **Voice sessions** — available in-app, in the car, anywhere

The platform is already live at coachsmb.netlify.app with a full library, onboarding flow, coach roster, and session engine.

**The Opportunity**

The AI coaching market sits at the intersection of two high-growth categories: enterprise L&D ($45B, growing 12% YoY) and AI productivity tools (growing 40%+ YoY). Coach AI targets the underserved mid-market — teams too small for enterprise coaching contracts, too serious for YouTube tutorials.

**Traction**

- Live product with full feature stack
- Inbound reseller interest prior to paid distribution
- Acquisition conversations active

**Ask**

We're raising a pre-seed round to fund go-to-market, expand the coach roster, and close the reseller pipeline. The product is built. The signal is real. We're looking for the right partner to accelerate.
